About Us

Building sustainable ventures that create value, opportunities, and positive impact.

Northern Holdings Nig. Ltd is a diversified holding company established to build, own, and grow sustainable businesses across critical sectors of the Nigerian economy, with a strategic focus on healthcare and technology.

We operate as a parent organization, providing structure, governance, and long-term direction to our subsidiaries while allowing each business to function independently with operational excellence. Our approach is deliberate: we prioritize value creation, scalability, and impact over short-term gains.

Northern Holdings was founded on the belief that strong institutions—not isolated ventures—drive lasting development. By combining traditional business discipline with modern innovation, we aim to create enterprises that solve real problems, generate employment, and contribute meaningfully to economic growth, particularly in Northern Nigeria.

Our expansion model is simple and disciplined: launch one well-structured business each year, ensure sustainability, then scale. This measured growth strategy allows us to manage risk, maintain quality, and build a resilient business portfolio for the future.

Investment & Strategy

Northern Holdings Nig. Ltd follows a disciplined and sustainable growth strategy, designed to maximize returns, manage risk, and create long-term value for partners, investors, and communities.

Growth Model

We launch one new business every January, alternating between healthcare and technology ventures, until at least 2030. Each business is carefully planned, market-tested, and financially structured to ensure sustainability before expansion. This measured approach allows us to scale responsibly while maintaining operational excellence across all subsidiaries.

Strategic Goals

Build a diversified portfolio of high-impact businesses in healthcare and technology.

Strengthen community engagement and trust through essential services and innovative solutions.

Leverage cross-synergies between subsidiaries to optimize operations and brand visibility.

Position Northern Holdings Nig. Ltd as a leading investment-ready group in Northern Nigeria.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our Company

What is Northern Holdings Nig. Ltd?

Northern Holdings Nig. Ltd is a diversified holding company that builds, owns, and manages businesses across healthcare, technology and Agriculture, with a focus on long-term value creation and community impact.

What sectors does Northern Holdings operate in?

We currently focus on two core sectors:

  1. Healthcare through N-Medix Ltd
  2. Technology & Digital Solutions through N-Sphere Innovates Ltd
  3. Agricultural Products and Services through NorthStar Agri Ltd.
Future expansion may include other strategic sectors aligned with our vision.

What companies currently operate under Northern Holdings Nig. Ltd?

We currently operate in the following Companies:

  1. Northern Medicine Store (launching January 2026) Under N-Medix Ltd
  2. Northern Creative Solutions Under N-Sphere Innovates Ltd
What is your growth strategy?

We follow a disciplined expansion model—launching one new business every January, alternating between healthcare and technology ventures, with a focus on sustainability before scaling.

Where does Northern Holdings operate?

Our operations are primarily based in Northern Nigeria particularly Katsina State, with digital services reaching clients nationwide and beyond.

Are you open to partnerships or investments?

Yes. Northern Holdings Nig. Ltd welcomes strategic partnerships, co-investments, and collaborations with individuals and organizations that share our commitment to innovation, growth, and impact.

How does Northern Holdings ensure sustainability?

We prioritize strong governance, regulatory compliance, prudent financial management, and reinvestment of profits to ensure each business is stable and scalable.

How can I contact or work with Northern Holdings Nig. Ltd?

You can reach us through our Contact Us page for business inquiries, partnerships, or collaboration opportunities.

What makes Northern Holdings different?

We are institution-driven, not hustle-driven. Our focus is on structure, discipline, long-term planning, and real impact, rather than short-term gains.
